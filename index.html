My Belief

I believe that when we depart from this world, there are many things we leave behind, like our legacy, our memories, tangible projects.

In the financial aspect, we can leave EITHER of the 2 things: Debt or Wealth. They cannot co-exist. Majority of Filipino families fall into debt because of high costs of healthcare.

To avoid debt, it's either you pay off everything before you leave, or someone else settle it on your behalf. The key to achieve this is through Personal Finance.

Why Personal Finance?

Personal Finance will dictate where your money should go, instead of wondering where it went.

One of the foundations of Personal Finance is Health Insurance. Health insurance is a form of financial service that pays for medical expenses in the midst of an illness or when health calls for it.
